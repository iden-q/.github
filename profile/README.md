## ⚛️ Who we are

**IdenQ** is a deep-tech security company from Spain working at the intersection of **post-quantum cryptography, digital identity, and digital asset protection**. Our multidisciplinary team brings together specialists in cryptography, bioengineering, security architecture, and Web2/Web3 engineering.

Our thesis is simple:

> Everything encrypted today with classical algorithms can be **harvested now and decrypted later**, the moment a cryptographically relevant quantum computer arrives. Long-lived secrets — keys, identities, inheritances — need quantum-safe protection *today*, not on Q-Day.

So we build systems where **hybrid encryption (classical + post-quantum)**, **zero-knowledge proofs**, and **threshold access** (no single party ever holds the full key) are the default, not an afterthought.

## 🔐 What we build

| | |
|---|---|
| 🛡️ **IdenQ Shield** | Duress-resistant protection for digital assets. A decoy-wallet architecture: under coercion, a duress passphrase reveals a plausible wallet while the real one stays cryptographically hidden — and unprovable. |
| 🧬 **IdenQ Legacy** | Quantum-safe digital inheritance. Heirs prove who they are with DNA-based verification, zero-knowledge proofs validate identity without exposing raw data, and decryption keys are split so no single party ever has full access. |
| ⚙️ **Quantum-safe core** | The hybrid cryptographic engine underneath: classical primitives (AES-256, elliptic curves) wrapped with NIST-standardized post-quantum schemes (ML-KEM / ML-DSA), designed for crypto-agility as standards evolve. |

## 🌍 What we're bringing to open source

Post-quantum migration is too big a problem for closed doors. The whole industry has to re-key the internet in the next decade — and that only works in the open. As we grow, we're committing to publish and maintain open tooling in the field we work in:

- 🔎 **Quantum-readiness scanning** — tools that audit codebases and dependencies for quantum-vulnerable cryptography (RSA, ECDSA, static DH…) and map a migration path to PQC.
- 🧩 **Hybrid-crypto building blocks** — practical, well-documented reference implementations for combining classical and post-quantum schemes (KEMs, signatures, envelope formats) without foot-guns.
- 🕵️ **Zero-knowledge identity patterns** — proving *who you are* without revealing *what you are*: ZK-based verification flows for sensitive identity data.
- 📚 **Knowledge base** — engineer-friendly guides on PQC standards (FIPS 203/204/205), harvest-now-decrypt-later threat modeling, and crypto-agility, in English and Spanish.

Our open source principles:

- **Security by transparency** — cryptography you can't inspect is cryptography you can't trust.
- **Standards first** — we build on NIST PQC standards and open formats, not proprietary lock-in.
- **Docs are part of the product** — every project ships with threat models and honest limitations.
